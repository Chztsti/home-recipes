Sausage and Veg Bake

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons cumin seeds
  • 2 teaspoons coriander seeds/li>
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 2 teaspoons chilli flakes
  • 500 g Sausages (pork)
  • 2-3 potatoes
  • 1 large red pepper
  • 1 cauliflower
  • 1 punnet cherry tomatoes
  • (optional) 1 red onion

Method:

  1. Pan roast the cumin and coriander seeds untill fragrant. Squish them with a mortar and pestle.
  2. Pan roast the rest of the spices untill fragrant and add to the cumin and coriander.
  3. Chop up the veg. Oil a baking tray(s) and distribute the veg, adding a sprinkle of oil and some salt.
  4. Legacy version: Fry the sausage until mostly cooked. Then add the veg to the baking tray(s).
    New version: Cut the sausage into bite sized pieces and add to the veg trays directly. (Don't bite the raw sausage).
  5. Sow the spice mix unto the veg/sausage mix.
  6. Bake that sucker at 200 °C until it's ready.
