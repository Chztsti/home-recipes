Sausage and Veg Bake

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons cumin seeds
  • 2 teaspoons coriander seeds/li>
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 2 teaspoons chilli flakes
  • 500 g Sausages (pork)
  • 2-3 potatoes
  • 1 large red pepper
  • 1 cauliflower
  • 1 punnet cherry tomatoes
  • (optional) 1 red onion

Method:

  1. Pan roast the cumin and coriander seeds untill fragrant. Squish them with a mortar and pestle.
  2. Pan roast the rest of the spices untill fragrant and add to the cumin and coriander.
  3. Wash the potatoes, and parboil them (10-15 min). When cooled, cut them into 1 cm thick slices and halve again into half moons.
  4. Chop up the rest of the veg. Oil a baking tray(s) and distribute all the veg into the baking tray(s), adding a sprinkle of oil and some salt.
  5. Fry the sausage until mostly cooked. Cut them into bite sized rounds and add them to the baking tray(s).
  6. Sow the spice mix unto the veg/sausage mix and mix it around a bit.
  7. Bake that sucker at 200 °C until it's ready (about 30 min).
