Butternut Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 Garlic cloves (squished or chopped)
  • ~1kg Butternut
  • 250 ml cream
  • 50-100 g Blue cheese or other cheesy time (Optional)
  • Salt + pepper to taste

Method:

If you have whole butternut, and you have some time, you can roast it whole for about 1 hr at 200 °C. Once cooled, scoop out the cooked butternut with a spoon. Otherwise, peel and chop the butternut and roast for about 30 min.

In a large pot, on medium heat, fry the onions. Add the garlic with some olive oil to absord the garlic oil. Fry for 1-2 min. Throw in the butternut and stir in cream. Spronkle salt and pepper to taste. Simmer and stir until smooshy. Meanwhile start cooking some pasta ; penne or tagliatelle work well. Add a splash or two of water/stock if needed to get desired consistency. Puree untill smooth (when cooled enough). Add cheese or other roasty treats (croutons, roasted seeds or other roast veg go well).
