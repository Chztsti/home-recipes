Bean Nachos

Serves:4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans black beans
  • 2 cans red beans
  • 2 cans tomatoes
  • handful of dry red lentils
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • 1 chopped red pepper
  • (Optional) 2-3 Cooked chicken pieces, deboned and shredded
  • Cheese

Spices

  • 3 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp chipotle powder
  • pinch of cinnamon

Method:

Fry the onions and red pepper in olive oil on medium heat. Add the garlic and fry for 1-2 min. Add the spices with a splash of oil and stir. When fragrant, add the beans (drained and washed). Stir to coat the beans. Add the tomato, and deglaze the pot. Add the lentils, with additional water if needed. Cook until smooshy.

Transfer half of the bean chilli to a ceramic baking tray. Stir the chicken into the chilli, if using. Layer nacho chips on top. Sprinkle grated cheese. Grill in the oven for 5-10 min (depending on your oven).
